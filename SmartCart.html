    Features

    • ✓AI-Powered Voice Commands: Add, remove, and clear items using natural language.
    • ✓Product Categorization: Items are automatically tagged with categories like "Produce" or "Dairy".
    • ✓Smart & Seasonal Suggestions: Get intelligent recommendations based on your list and the current season.
    • ✓Multi-Language Support: Speak in various languages, and the app will understand.
    • ✓Interactive List: Check off items as you shop, and they'll be saved for your next visit.

    How It Works

    1. Click the "Speak" Button: Grant microphone access if prompted. The button will pulse to show it's listening.
    2. State Your Command: Speak clearly, e.g., "Add two cartons of milk and a loaf of bread".
    3. AI Processing: The app sends your speech to Gemini for analysis. It understands quantities, multiple items, and even assigns a category.
    4. List Updates: Your list is instantly updated. Smart and seasonal suggestions will appear based on the items you've added.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is SmartCart free to use?

    Yes, SmartCart is completely free for personal use. It utilizes the browser's built-in Web Speech API and the Gemini API, which has a free tier suitable for regular use.

    What browsers are supported?

    For the best experience, please use a modern browser that supports the Web Speech API, such as Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Safari.

    How is my data used?

    Your voice commands are sent to the Gemini AI for processing to understand your request. Your shopping list data is stored locally in your browser's storage and is not saved on any external servers.

    Can I use it offline?

    The core speech-to-text and AI processing require an active internet connection. You can view your last saved list offline, but you cannot add or remove items using your voice without being connected.
